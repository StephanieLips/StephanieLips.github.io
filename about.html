Me

Hi there!

Life is fun. Life is difficult. Life is all kind of things.

That's why I started this blog. I like talking. A lot.

And I have weird ideas. Now it's time to involve other people.


You might ask: Who are you anyway?

Legit question. Let me answer. I'm a 27-year-old living in western Germany.

I'm a project manager in a marketing agency organizing the creation and edtiors of

stuff. I programmed this site on my own because I thought it might be beneficial.


I really do love my job but I have various interests whatsoever. I love reading and watching

a lot about food, diet and alternative concepts of living or working.

Just some general facts in between: I'm an atheist and a sceptic at the same time making

my life and interacting with people really interesting. Sometimes it helps to view things

from a thousand points of view.


If you like this blog, please don't bother following me on Facebook or Instagram. Or write

me an email.


Best wishes!
